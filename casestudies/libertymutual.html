Quotes for a Cure

In 2004, four-year-old cancer patient Alexandra (Alex) Scott opened a lemonade stand to raise money for the fight against childhood cancer. Alex died in 2008. Since then, families and corporations have pitched in to raise over $150 million.

We helped Liberty Mutual join the fight with Quotes for a Cure, a workplace program that promised a donation to Alex's foundation each time an employee requested a quote on home or auto insurance.
